# 01 — 浮——岛
## Meta
- Project: 海上钻井平台酒店改造设计
- Year: 2024
- Award: 米兰设计周全国三等奖
- Category: Spatial Design
## EN
The project is located on a retired offshore drilling platform within the Bohai Chengbei Oilfield. The design aims to transform the original oil platform framework into a marine complex integrating residence, ecological restoration, education, and energy self-sufficiency. This initiative not only compensates for marine ecosystems but also represents a new attempt at harmonious coexistence between humans and nature. A key innovation in the residential experience is that living units can be freely raised or lowered into the seawater, allowing visitors to move freely on the sea surface. The FLOATING ISLANDS design is inspired by floating islands: the top features a forest and activity center, the middle layer houses the living units, the "roots" form the dining area, and the first floor uses mirrored glass to reflect the surroundings, creating a sense of architectural levitation.
## CN
该项目地点位于渤海埕北油田内一退役海上钻井平台。本设计旨在通过利用原有的石油平台框架结构，将其转变为一个集居住、生态恢复、教育和能源自给于一体的海上综合体。这不仅是对海洋生态的一种补偿，也是人类与自然和谐共存的一个新尝试。居住体验创新点在于居住单元可以自由升降放到海水中，旅客可以在海面上自由活动。FLOATING ISLANDS造型来源于浮空岛屿，顶部是森林和活动中心，岛的夹层是居住单元，根系是餐厅部分，第一层通过镜面玻璃包裹反射环境来营造建筑的漂浮感。

# 02 — Feces with Earth
## Meta
- Project: 新消费理念及可持续视角下的粪便卫生展馆设计
- Year: 2024
- Award: 中国人居环境设计学年奖-优秀奖
- Category: Spatial Design
## EN
We re-examine urban waste, from Freud’s idea that 'excrement is gold' to Marx’s thought, considering that feces are never filthy or dirty, but resources that can be recycled. They no longer remain in a single form but continue to move, interact in multiple ways, and cycle in complex manners. Therefore, in the so-called natural world, very little feces remain in their original state. However, in today’s human society, culturally, they are seen as things we do not wish to see.
This exhibition brings visitors the concepts of health, sustainability, and eco-friendliness in the composition and utilization of feces from the perspective of sustainability and new consumption. The exhibition hall incorporates green and silver as the main colors, and uses multimedia, human-computer interaction, and the collision and application of new materials as the core of its design to create a completely new experience. The narrative expression brought by the spatial layout stimulates people to re-examine, reflect on, and even resonate with sanitation culture and the related use of feces.
## CN
我们重新审视城市的废物，从弗洛伊德的"粪便即是金子"到马克思的思想，粪便从不是肮脏污秽，而是可以循环利用的资源。它们不再停留在一种形式，而是继续移动，以多种方式相互影响，并以复杂的方式循环。因此，在所谓的自然世界中，很少有粪便保持原样。 然而，在当今的人类社会中，在文化上它们被视为我们不希望看到的东西。

该展馆从可持续、新消费视角下将粪便组成、粪便利用中的健康、可持续、绿色理念带给游客。该展厅融入绿色、银色、作为主色调，以多媒体，人机交互，新材料的碰撞与运用为设计核心打造全新体验，以及空间布局所带来的叙事性表达引发人们对卫生文化及相关粪便利用的重新审视与思考甚至共鸣。

# 03 — 嘬味ZUOWEI
## Meta
- Project: 商业空间设计
- Year: 2025
- Award: —
- Category: Spatial & Brand Design
## EN
This project builds a personalized brand of shared kitchens for university campuses. It aims to establish a campus community service platform integrating healthy dining, cultural exchange, innovation and entrepreneurship. Fully-equipped cooking and dining facilities are provided for college students to create a free space for cooking and communication. Students only need to purchase their preferred ingredients, as complete home-style kitchen and dining appliances are available on site. Meanwhile, the service saves young people the trouble of cleaning cookware, bowls and chopsticks.
The shared kitchen is divided into multiple functional zones, including general cooking area, hot pot area, baking area and social lounge. Furthermore, the project adopts the "Community Plus" concept. The combination of offline and online communities breaks down barriers in social interaction and daily life. Through diverse events and brand influence, it develops a social-oriented, sustainable shared kitchen community.
## CN
本项目打造高校共享厨房的个性化品牌，旨在打造一个集健康饮食、文化交流与创新创业于一体的校园社区服务平台，为大学生提供完备的厨房设施和就餐设备，营造一个自由烹饪交流的空间。同学们只要买来自己喜欢的菜肴，所有像家里一样的厨房设备和餐厅设备都一应俱全，同时也为年轻人省去了清理厨具和碗筷的麻烦与困扰。共享厨房划分成多个功能区，烹饪区，火锅区，烘焙区，社交区等等，除此之外，该项目介入 “社区 +” 理念，线下 + 线上社区打破社交边界，生活边界，以多元化活动和品牌效应打造社交型、可持续的共享厨房社区。

# 04 — Prometheus
## Meta
- Project: 月球核能概念建筑设计
- Year: 2025
- Award: —
- Category: Architectural Design
## EN
Against the backdrop of an increasingly acute conflict between environmental protection and development, drawing on cutting-edge contemporary research outcomes across aerospace material science, lunar soil survey data, optoelectronic transmission technology, microphysics, and matter extraction technology, this design integrates the traits of the slime mold algorithm. It maps spatial parameters, terrain information and detection data to the environment, obstacles and food factors defined in the slime mold algorithm respectively, and leverages the algorithm to calculate the optimal collection path. Afterwards, the helium harvesting unit robot executes a set of behavioral sequences: deploying guide rails, advancing to target terminals, receiving unit replenishment from the root base, and continuously extending and laying additional guide rails. By adopting an "organic growth" architectural paradigm, the system achieves full material utilization and cyclic material recovery. This report hereby presents a forward-looking solution to address humanity’s energy crisis for the next generation.
## CN
在环境与发展之间矛盾日益尖锐的大背景下，依托航空航天材料科学、月壤探测数据、光电传输技术、微观物理学与物质提取技术等领域当下前沿研究成果，结合黏菌算法的特性，将空间、地形与探测数据分别映射为黏菌算法中的环境、障碍物与食物因子；依托该算法求解采集最优路径，随后氦采集单元机器人遵循这套行为逻辑：释放导向轨道→行进至终点→根部补给单元→持续延展铺设导向轨道。依托建筑的 “有机生长” 模式，实现材料的充分利用与循环回收。递交这份应对下一代人类能源危机的未来方案。
